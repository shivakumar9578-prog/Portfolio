## 🎓 Customization Guide:

Want to modify your portfolio?

### To Change Content:
- Edit `index.html` with any text editor
- Update your information
- Replace `assets/profile.jpg` with your photo
- Replace `assets/resume.pdf` with your resume

### To Change Colors:
- Open `style.css`
- Find `:root` section at top
- Change color values:
  ```css
  --accent:#38bdf8;      /* Change this to your color */
  --accent2:#8b5cf6;     /* And this */
  ```

### To Add More Content:
- Follow same HTML patterns
- Keep mobile responsiveness in mind
- Test on phone before deploying
